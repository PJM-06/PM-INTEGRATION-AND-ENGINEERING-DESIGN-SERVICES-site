About Us

PM Integration and Engineering Design Services is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary engineering firm in the Philippines dedicated to providing innovative, reliable, and code-compliant electrical and electronics solutions. Our expertise spans across residential, commercial, and industrial projects, offering end-to-end services from conceptual planning to technical documentation and implementation support.

We specialize in Electrical Planning & Design, including load analysis (voltage drop, short-circuit evaluation, and protection coordination), Power Distribution Systems (panelboards, riser diagrams, feeder schedules, PEC-compliant layouts), and Lighting Layout & Simulation using Dialux and Relux. We also prepare Bill of Quantities (BOQ), As-Built Drawings, and comprehensive Technical Specifications for compliance and accuracy.

Beyond core electrical works, we provide complete Integrated Systems solutions: Structured Cabling & Data Networks, Fire Detection & Alarm Systems (FDAS), CCTV & Security Systems, Access Control Systems, Public Address & Audio-Visual Systems, and Telecommunication Infrastructure.

Since 2021, PM Integration has successfully handled diverse projects such as residential electrical installations, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and academic prototype developments. Each project is approached with precision, ensuring compliance with Philippine Electrical Code (PEC), international standards, and client-specific requirements.

Guided by our core values of quality, integrity, innovation, and safety, we strive to be a trusted partner in building sustainable, efficient, and future-ready systems that empower communities and businesses alike.
